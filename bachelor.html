Who is this for?

This page is for students who want to start a Bachelor degree in Italy and need support with admission and scholarships.

Required documents

  • High school certificate with grades, addressed to Italy and translated.
  • Language certificate B2 in Italian or English.
  • TOLC online admission test (for most majors).
  • IGCSE / American Diploma: 12 years of schooling or 1-year foundation.

How DiboVentures helps you

  • Choosing the right program and university.
  • Preparing and submitting applications.
  • Guidance for language & TOLC exams.
  • Support with scholarships, visa and housing.
